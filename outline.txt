- Actionpad
	- What is Actionpad?
		- [closed] **(1) A notepad that takes action**
			- [closed] You can take notes like you might in Notion or Workflowy
				- Bullets and sub-bullets. Hide or reveal entire sections. Move things around with ease. /commands
			- [closed] And you can take action
				- [closed] Any bullet can be "run" as a prompt by your AI (Codex by default)
					- [closed] When you run a bullet, the app sends that bullet to the runtime
						- Cmd-Enter sends the focused bullet text, nearby outline context, and any mentioned files to a localhost Node runtime.
						- The runtime talks to Codex through the Codex SDK, streams events back over a websocket, and the UI turns those events into chat, spinners, checks, and errors.
					- [closed] The AI adds sub-bullets as responses.
						- Codex is instructed to end with exactly one Actionpad JSON patch: append child bullets, update bullet text, delete bullets, or batch those actions.
					- The full chat thread for that bullet is accessible in the sidebar
		- [closed] **(2) A customizable app**
			- you can modify it yourself by writing your idea as a bullet and hitting cmd-enter
			- actionpad was mostly made within actionpad!
			- try it out: hit cmd-enter on this line and actionpad will remove the theolabs branding at the bottom of the screen.
